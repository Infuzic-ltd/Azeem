A) Introduction

Embark on an inspiring journey of knowledge and growth with Educiza, a meticulously designed HTML template tailored for educational institutions, online courses, and e-learning platforms. Whether you're an academy, university, school, or independent educator, Educiza offers a seamless blend of modern design and powerful functionality to elevate your digital presence.

With its intuitive and visually captivating layout, Educiza ensures an engaging user experience, allowing students, educators, and administrators to navigate effortlessly. Showcase your courses, faculty, and success stories with clarity and professionalism while maintaining a sleek and dynamic aesthetic that aligns with contemporary educational standards.

Unlock the full potential of your institution with Educiza’s robust features, including course management, student testimonials, event highlights, and blog sections for sharing valuable insights. Its fully responsive design guarantees a flawless browsing experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring accessibility for learners worldwide.

Optimized for SEO and compatible with all modern browsers, Educiza helps you reach a broader audience, attract students, and establish a reputable online presence. Whether you're launching an online learning portal or enhancing an existing institution's website, Educiza provides the perfect foundation to showcase your commitment to education and innovation.

It contains 3 Home Page layouts and about page layout and 26 valid HTML5 page templates designs, different Blog page styles and shop pages. Educiza template features are coded with Bootstrap v4.6.2, HTML5 & CSS3 and unlimited color schemes. It’s compatible with all modern browsers and search engine friendly. So showcase your artworks and services with this awesome template!

B) How to Edit HTML Files?

We have included 26 custom HTML templates like Home Page, About, Courses, Team and Contact etc. Please open any HTML files with a text editor like Dreamweaver, Notepad or Notepad++ and edit any lines what you want.

C) How to Edit CSS Files?

We have included some custom CSS styles like style.css (default). Please open any CSS files with a text editor like Dreamweaver, Notepad or Notepad++ and edit any lines what you want. For example if you want to edit your banner color open style.css and look at ".banner-con" for banner image and change your image.

 

D) How to use Color Schemes?

Please open style.css file from Educiza/assets/css folder with a text editor and build your own colors. #ffffff this is our primary color, you can search and replace all to your new color code.

 

E) How to use On Load Animation?

We are aos.js on load animation for our website. you can edit them by simply adding or changeing the predefined classes name.
